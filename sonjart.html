sonjART Gallery

Online Art Gallery & Curation Platform based in Switzerland.

🎨 Project Overview

sonjART is a refined online art gallery founded by Sonja Schlagel, a Netherlands-born art curator based in Switzerland. The platform bridges the world of international fine art and passionate collectors.

"Furniture fills a house, but art gives it a soul."

We transformed the traditional gallery experience into a digital-first platform, making it easy to discover, explore, and acquire original paintings from a curated selection of global artists.

✨ Key Features

  • Curated Art Collections: Beautifully organized gallery with transparent pricing in CHF.
  • Artist Profiles: Dedicated pages for international talent like Nicole Laceur and Henk Vierveijzer.
  • Events & Exhibitions: Dynamic section for pop-up gallery shows and soirées.
  • Personal Art Curation: Personalized services to help clients find the perfect piece.

🖌️ Design Philosophy

The website carries a clean, minimal aesthetic that lets the art take center stage. The layout is elegant and gallery-inspired — with high-quality imagery, structured navigation, and a warm, personal tone that reflects Sonja's curatorial vision.
