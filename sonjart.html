sonjART Gallery

International Fine Art curation & collection platform.

Project Overview

sonjART is a refined online gallery founded by Sonja Schlagel, a Netherlands-born art curator based in Switzerland. The platform bridges the world of international fine art and passionate collectors—making it easy to discover and acquire original paintings from global artists.

Core System

We created a digital experience that reflects Sonja's motto: "Furniture fills a house, but art gives it a soul." The site focuses on high-resolution presentation, detailed artist biographies, and a seamless path to ownership.

Key Features

  • Curated Art Collections
  • Artist Portfolios
  • Modern Gallery UI
  • Secure Acquisition path
